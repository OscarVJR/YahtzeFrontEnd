Welcome to Yahtzee Lite!
Click the 'play' button to start the game! You win if you roll a large straight! (either any combination of 1,2,3,4,5 or 2,3,4,5,6)
You can roll a maximum of 3 times! Before that, you can choose which die to keep and the rest will roll!



If a die is kept, it is not rolled. "Keeper" dice will stay the value they are at.
